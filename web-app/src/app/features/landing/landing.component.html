What is Mobflow?

A real-world SaaS built to demonstrate professional-grade software engineering — from architecture to deployment.

Team Workspaces

Organize your work in collaborative workspaces. Invite teammates, assign roles and manage projects together.

Task Management

Boards, lists and tasks with priorities, deadlines and assignments. Everything your team needs to stay on track.

Real-time Chat

Team chat with direct messages and channels. Built with WebSocket for instant communication. Coming soon.
