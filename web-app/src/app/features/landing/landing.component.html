What is Mobflow?

A real-world SaaS built to demonstrate professional-grade software engineering from architecture to deployment.

Team Workspaces

Organize work in collaborative workspaces with members, roles, invitations and public join codes.

Task Management

Boards, lists and tasks with priorities, deadlines, assignees, analytics and workspace summaries.

Notification Pipeline

Kafka-driven notifications backed by MongoDB, unread counters and email confirmation delivery.
